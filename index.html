About Me

Hi, I’m Labinot Sherifi, a Full Stack Developer with a passion for building seamless, scalable web applications. I have a strong foundation in both front-end and back-end technologies, and I specialize in creating dynamic, user-friendly solutions. I’ve worked on a wide variety of projects, from ERP systems and marketplace applications to Shopify integrations. With expertise in React.js, Node.js, AWS, and a range of databases, I’m equipped to handle all stages of web development, from design to deployment.

My journey as a developer has been shaped by my experience working on real-world projects like integrating with Shopify’s API to create applications that facilitate product publishing and inventory management. I’ve worked on optimizing workflows for clients and building APIs and backend services that integrate with complex systems.

I hold a BSc in Computer Engineering from the University of Prishtina and have hands-on experience with cutting-edge technologies such as React.js, Node.js, MySQL, AWS (Amplify, Elastic Beanstalk, EC2, RDS), and more. I believe in continuously improving my skills, staying updated with the latest tech trends, and writing clean, maintainable code that delivers long-term value.
